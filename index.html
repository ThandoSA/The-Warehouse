About the Space

More Than a Venue — It's a Home

The Warehouse is a co-working and collaborative space located in Lorentzville, Johannesburg, built to serve Kingdom-centred NGOs and nonprofits who are working to transform their communities.

Our vision is to become a gathering place for people and organisations who love Jesus, love the city, and are passionate about seeing the Kingdom of Christ flourish in Johannesburg — a space where they are able to plant deep roots and build something lasting.

Whether you're hosting a board meeting, a community fundraiser, or a creative workshop, The Warehouse provides the space, atmosphere, and support to make it meaningful.

"Not just a venue — it's a Kingdom-driven community space." The Warehouse Team
